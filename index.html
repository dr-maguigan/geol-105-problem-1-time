GEOL 105 - Introduction to Geology


Problem 1: Geologic Time

The purpose of this activity is to apply logic and the material discussed in lecture to a problem. In this case, you are trying to help someone with no prior knowledge of the Earth's age to understand how scientists arrived at an estimate of 4.5-4.7 billion years old.


Problem Start: Your goal is to explain to this person how scientists arrived at the current estimate for the age of the Earth.
